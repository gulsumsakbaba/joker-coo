Who Is Joker?

One of the most psychopathic villains in comic book history, the Joker is Batman's nemesis. The world's most popular villain, the Joker was created by Bob Kane, Jerry Robinson and Bill Finger, who were inspired by the Joker card found in a deck of cards. He first appeared in a comic book published on 25 April 1940, and his appearance was worthy of his character. In a live radio broadcast, he recounted his murders one by one, challenging Batman and the police and setting off a chase that has yet to end.

Why does it look like that, Joker?

I used to be a lab worker until I decided to steal a million dollars and retire! That's how I became the Red Hood! I finally achieved my goal by robbing the Monarch Playing Card Company. Thanks to the oxygen tank in my helmet, I was able to swim under the chemical waste pool and escape with ease. When the criminal returns home, he is horrified to find that the chemicals in the waste pool have turned his skin chalky white, his hair green and his lips red. Only later does he accept this change in himself and embarks on a life of crime. One of the most iconic characters in the comic book world is born: The Joker.
